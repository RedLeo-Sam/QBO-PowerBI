This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the QBO to Power BI Connector ("Application"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data.

1. Information We Collect

Our Application is designed to be a secure bridge between your QuickBooks Online (QBO) account and Power BI. We do not collect, store, or sell any of your personally identifiable information or financial data.

The Application requires you to sign in to your QBO account to grant us temporary, limited access to your data. This access is solely for the purpose of retrieving the information necessary to generate a Power BI dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

The Application uses your QuickBooks Online data for one purpose only: to retrieve and present it to you within a Power BI dashboard. We do not store your data on our servers. The data passes through our application's temporary memory and is never saved to a database or disk.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data during its transit from QuickBooks Online to Power BI. All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Since we do not store your data, the risk of data breaches on our end is minimized.

4. Third-Party Services

This Application relies on the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Intuit QuickBooks Online: Your financial data is managed and secured by Intuit. You should review their privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.
  • Microsoft Power BI: The data visualization and dashboarding are provided by Microsoft. You should review their privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at saad@binkhalid.net.
